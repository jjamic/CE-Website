Typical engagement points

Clients typically engage us when...

These are the moments where the cost of getting the engineering wrong is highest — and where our judgment adds the most value.

Have an idea but don't know where to start
We can map a path from first idea to manufacture — or start with a free 15-min call if you just want a frank first opinion.
Industrial design is complete but engineering is unresolved
Structural validation, DFM review, and manufacturing feasibility before development investment.
Approaching tooling and need a final engineering review
Pre-production audit before committing to tooling spend — when the cost of a redesign is highest.
The product feels over-engineered or too expensive to manufacture
Component consolidation, geometry simplification, and material review to reduce cost without compromising performance.
A prototype failed and root cause is unclear
Structural analysis and failure mode investigation to understand why and what to change before rebuilding.
Need structural validation before production or compliance testing
FEA and load case development to validate performance against applicable standards before committing to volume.
